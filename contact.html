ABOUT US

Chick'n Fries began operations in the Saudi Arabia, Chick'n Fries offers an array of taste to its customers in chicken, beef,vegetables or fish. Each new improvising has been extensively tested to ensure that it complies with the Chick'n Fries concept, its value for quality and taste. Our heritage, spirit and origins are authentically Jeddha.
